A pilot project for a community currency network

Open money is a platform for creating community currencies. It enables exchange without the use of conventional money, as well as many other functions like reputation currencies, loyalty currencies, time dollars, demurage currencies, etc...

This site a is fully operable demonstration of an open money currency service provider. You can log in and give it a try, and you can also download and install our open source software be a currency service provider service for your communities.
